Behind the Kitchen

At CC Cafe, we provide top-quality, authentic handmade cakes and pastries freshy baked every morning with love. We focus on healthy ingredients making each bite simply delicious. We use a FREE FROM range: Gluten, Dairy, Soy and Refined Sugar, as we want our customers to feel like they can indulge into delicious sweet treats! Our aim is to infuse our ingredients with superfoods from around the world, such as Raw Coca, Acai, Maca, Spirulina and Goji Berries, to give you that extra boost in life! All our ingredients are 100% wholesome with no artifical ingredients.
